Developing

With the project fully initialized, you are free to start coding!

You may add files to the project yourself, or click Gen Template

Clicking Gen Template will create a file main.rs that will serve as an

entry point for development. To access it, and any other files, switch to

the File Explorer tab. Iron Coder will display useful information for

your board, including any available crates!

Once you are satisfied with your changes, press build project to compile

and load onto board to flash your changes to your board of choice.

For board-specific flashing instructions, see the Supported Boards section of

the homepage.

That concludes this tutorial, and we hope you enjoy Iron Coder!
